Product Development and Start-Up Business Models for Energy Professionals

Course Overview: The Product Development and Start-Up Business Models for Energy Professionals course is designed to empower energy professionals with practical knowledge and skills to thrive in the evolving energy landscape. Participants will explore strategies for identifying market needs, designing innovative energy products, and crafting sustainable start-up business models. The course will emphasize frameworks for fostering profitability while addressing environmental and societal challenges, equipping professionals to lead in a competitive and rapidly transforming industry. Through case studies, interactive sessions, and actionable insights, attendees will gain the tools needed to drive innovation and create impactful energy solutions.
